Did you know . . . .
  1. Breakfast is served FREE to all students each school day.
  2. Please continue to promote school breakfast with all your students.
  3. Healthy eating options such as fresh fruit, whole wheat bread,
  4. salads and vegetables are served daily in the school cafeteria.
  5. New USDA guidelines now require a fruit or vegetable be served
  6. as part of each student's lunch. Milk is optional and is not a required
  7. meal component.
  8. The school cafeteria sells lunch for adults and non-students at the
  9. low price of $3.00 and breakfast for only $2.00.

INSURANCE FOR PERSONAL ELECTRONIC DEVICES IN USE AT SCHOOL / SEGURO PARA DISPOSITIVOS ELECTRONICOS PERSONALES
M-DCPS has examined the need to insure mobile devices at a reasonable cost. The Worth Group has established an online portal to access coverage costs for a district HP ElitePad tablet or the HP 210G1 notebook with a zero deductible. Students, teachers and staff are eligible to insure district or personal devices. Coverage includes accidental damage, cracked screens, liquid submersion, theft, vandalism, fire, flood, natural disasters and power surge by lightning. A quote for coverage can be found at https://my.worthavegroup.com/dadeschoolsfl. For those who do not have internet access, below this briefing is the paper application. For other electronic devices (BYOD), a separate portal https://my.worthavegroup.com/dadbyod is available for devices including cameras, cellphones, E-Readers, gaming systems, iPads, iPhones, and laptops. Prices and deductibles vary depending on the value and device. Parents may also access this link. For questions regarding coverage contact, Mr. Charlie Chiamardas, Account Executive, Worth Ave. Group at 561-707-3323 or via email Chazz@WorthAveGroup.com .
